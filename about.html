Our Story

We started The Knife Guys in 2003 with one goal — to give people a place where they could actually hold a blade before buying it. Not scroll through photos online. Not guess at the weight and balance. Actually feel it in their hand.

Over 20 years later, that's still what we do. We carry the brands people trust — Benchmade, Case, William Henry, Buck, Kershaw — and we take the time to show you the differences in function, build, and metal. Not just the price tag.

We've grown to two locations — Amarillo and Lubbock — and added custom engraving at both stores. Whether you need a hunting knife, a kitchen workhorse, a collector's display piece, or a custom-engraved gift, we've got it. And if we don't have it in stock, we can order it.

The right person, for the right blade.
